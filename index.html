Prioritizer

This tool is supposed to guide you through the difficult process of prioritizing an item list by divding it into a series of binary decisions that are easier to make.

1. Add Items

Paste a list of items (one item per line) here and click on Add Items below to continue.

2. Identified Items

Here you can find all items that have been recognized by the tool.

3. Choices

In order to start the priorization process click on Create Choices below.

4. Evaluation

Having prioritized all items, you may now review the evaluation revealing a prioritized version of your initial item list by clicking on Evaluation.
